Privacy Policy – Tiny Humans

Effective date: December 3, 2025

Tiny Humans is a completely offline, single-player arcade game. This privacy policy explains what data (if any) we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information whatsoever.

  • No account or login is required
  • No advertising ID is accessed
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No crash reporting or usage data is sent
  • No permissions are requested (no internet, no storage, no location, etc.)

2. Data Collection & Usage

The game runs 100% locally on your device. All game progress, settings, and data are stored only on your phone and are never transmitted anywhere.

3. Third-Party Services

This game does not use any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics providers, or social media integrations.

4. Children’s Privacy

Tiny Humans contains mild cartoon violence (pixel-art blood). It is rated Teen on the Google Play Store. The game does not collect any data from children or anyone else.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
